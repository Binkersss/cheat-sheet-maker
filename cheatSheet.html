Instructions:
  • Click "Add Tile" to create a new tile.
  • Click a tile to select it (highlighted in red), then click "Delete Selected" to remove it.
  • Drag tiles to move them, and resize from edges.
  • Tiles snap to a 20px grid.
  • Paste images into a selected tile using Ctrl+V or right-click paste.
  • Click "Print to PDF" to receive a PDF form of your cheat-sheet.
  • Click "Save" to download a snapshot of your current cheat-sheet. (THERE IS NO AUTOSAVE)
  • Click "Import File" to import a previously saved snapshot.
  • You can choose between:
    • An 8.5x11 sheet of paper
    • A 4x5 notecard
